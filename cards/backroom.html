Backroom Level 2

2023 · Unity game prototype

1 / 4

A short atmospheric Unity prototype inspired by the Backrooms and liminal spaces. The player explores a strange, oppressive environment, collects key cards, avoids an AI monster roaming the level, and unlocks the final exit door.

The project includes a short cinematic introduction, a compact handcrafted level built with ProBuilder, and a focus on mood, lighting, sound, and environmental tension.

What I built

  • Created the full level layout in Unity using ProBuilder
  • Designed the exploration flow around collecting key cards and unlocking the exit
  • Added an AI monster that searches for the player inside the level
  • Built a short cinematic introduction to set up the atmosphere
  • Tuned lighting, sound, and ambience to create tension and pacing
  • Explored liminal-space aesthetics through layout, scale, and environmental mood
