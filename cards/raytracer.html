Raytracer

2022 · Computer graphics project · ETH Zurich CG22

A computer graphics project developed for the CG22 course at ETH Zurich. The project implemented a custom path tracer and a final rendered scene designed to showcase the renderer's features.

I later submitted that final image to the ETH computer graphics section's Disney renderer competition. Students enter with a rendered scene; a shortlist is selected to present their work. I was selected and presented my renderer and final image to the section.

Key features

  • Texture mapping
  • Depth of field
  • Textured emitters
  • Custom mesh modeling
  • Intel Open Image Denoise integration
  • Normal mapping
  • Environment map lighting
  • Homogeneous participating media
  • Basic raytraced portals
  • Final rendered scene
