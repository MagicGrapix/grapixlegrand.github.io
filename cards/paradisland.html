Paradisland

2020 · Academic project · EPFL

An academic graphics project developed at EPFL. I contributed to the real-time rendering pipeline, with a focus on water and screen-space visual effects.

What I built

  • Water shader with pixel-style normal mapping, distortion, and animated surface detail
  • Screen-space reflections, including a planar reflection mode with an inverted scene render
  • Screen-space shadows
  • Outlining effect for stylized object separation
